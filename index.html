Psyche Asteroid

The NASA Psyche mission is a journey to 16 Psyche, a very special asteroid. It was discovered in 1852 by Italian astronomer Annibale de Gasparis. It’s the largest known M-type asteroid, which means it’s composed of high concentrations of metal!

It has a diameter of 140 miles. That’s longer than the distance from Phoenix to Tucson. It has a mass of 2.3 x 10^19, which is about 3,000 times less than the moon. The asteroid’s orbit is circular, and it resides in between Mars’ and Jupiter’s orbit
